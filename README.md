## About Me
As I began my Bachelor of Science in Cybersecurity and Information Assurance in 2024, my initial interest was in offensive security. As I progressed through A+, Network+, and Security+, I discovered my stronger interest is in governance, security architecture, and the structured protection of systems.

I started by hardening my own environment (separate admin accounts, removing unused services, deny-by-default firewall principles) and then focused on building baseline comparison workflows that detect configuration drift through scheduled scans—supporting configuration management and continuous monitoring.

## Current Focus

- Continuous monitoring & configuration drift detection
